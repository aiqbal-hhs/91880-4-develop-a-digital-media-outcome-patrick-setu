Paris Butter

This restaurant is a french style cuisine that sells modern takes on some french classics but using fresh ingredients that are sourced from local areas. The owner is Chef Nick Honeyman who has made a name for himself with titles such as one of the best restaurants and best chef in New Zealand. Him and his team of chefs work together to create very high quality food as well as an elegant dining experience. The interior of the restaurant has been designed to give a very refined and classy feel while eating. What they serve on their menu changes according to what seasonal produce is available to them which ensures that each time you visit, you won’t get bored of their food. Along with their mains, they also serve different kinds of desserts and some snacks. Paris butter only does dine in and not take away although they do offer the service of their team being private chefs that create and serve food from your home.

Paris butter is located at 166 Jervois Road, Herne Bay, Auckland and can be contacted through 09 376 5597. Their operating hours are 5:30 - late from Tuesday to Saturday and are closed on both Sunday and Monday.

Their website is https://parisbutter.co.nz/

Mt Albert BBQ Noodle House

The flavours of Mt Albert BBQ Noodle House is a Beijing style cuisine and sell quite a large variety of foods. They have all sorts of meats that can be cooked in different styles with vegetables served over rice, many kinds of noodles, and more. Their menu is very large with over almost 80 types of food available and are also vegetarian friendly. They sell noodle styled dishes, rice based dishes, and soups but they even sell bubble tea and some snacks like hot chips or chicken nuggets. This restaurant is well known for not only how good their food is but also how it has a comfortable atmosphere and how amazing their service that they provide to their customers is. Their food is served in very generous large portions and at a very affordable price. An option for takeaway is available and the parking is close and free which is a bonus.

Their address is 930 New North Road, Mount Albert, Auckland and their phone number is 09 815 3228.

Their website is https://mmtea.co.nz/browse/
